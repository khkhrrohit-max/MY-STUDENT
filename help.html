About MY STUDENT

MY STUDENT is an online student portal created to provide study materials, semester notes, previous year papers, academic calendars, and useful resources in one place. Our goal is to make learning easier and faster for every student.

How to Use

  1. Login into your account.
  2. Open Dashboard.
  3. Select Course.
  4. Choose Semester.
  5. Open PDF Notes.
  6. Download Study Material.
  7. Logout after use.
